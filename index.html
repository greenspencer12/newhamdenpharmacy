About Us

A Local Pharmacy
That Truly Cares

New Hamden Pharmacy is your trusted local pharmacy located at 1220 Whitney Ave, Hamden, CT. We focus on faster service and better care — because we believe your health deserves more than a chain pharmacy experience.

We offer prescription transfers, vaccinations, medication synchronization, refill reminders, and one-on-one pharmacist support. As a proud Health Mart partner, we combine the resources of a national network with the warmth of a local team.

  • Easy prescription transfers — we handle everything for you
  • We work directly with your doctors and all insurance providers
  • Proud member of the Hamden Regional Chamber of Commerce
  • Free delivery within 20 miles of our Hamden location
